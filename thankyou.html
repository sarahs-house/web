Thank You!

Dear Research Participants,

If you have participated in a research interview or survey, thank you so much for sharing your life story with me. It would be impossible to study society without hearing from everyone who forms it. Your experiences and expertise inspire me every day to be a better scholar and human being. No two people in this world are alike, and we are better off for it!

Thank you,
Sarah
